Welcome to  HackBMU 2.O !

Flagship event of 67th Milestone.

8th & 9th February 2019, BML Munjal University

About The Event

The event will host the best upcoming engineers and developers from across India to create mobile, web and hardware hacks for 24 hours. Bringing in students from all over India, HackBMU is committed to encourage and kickstart a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship and community among future hackers of India.

HackBMU seeks to keep innovation in the area through hackathon and also provide supportive environment for everyone to realize their dreams help them in developing their skills.
